Hello, my name is Chyi and I'm applying to General Assembly because I love to code! I recently discovered my passion for coding while at work. I grew tired of doing the same redundant tasks so I decided to learn how to automate my work by writing a script.

I'm incredibly persistent and self-motivated. I'm very excited at the prospect of attending General Assembly and feel I have what it takes to be a successful student. I believe in the success of the curriculum as well as the programs investment with each students success.
